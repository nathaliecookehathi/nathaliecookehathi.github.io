When were riddle events most popular?

Although our dataset includes riddles from between 1733 and 1971, the hottest point for riddling was from 1890 to 1920.

Scroll down to begin

Riddle Distribution

The hottest period of riddling was from the late 1800s to the early 1900s. More than 90% of the riddles from this data set were published between 1890 and 1920.

Focusing on a Period (1890-1920)

Let's focus in on that period, and see exactly what happened--particularly by moving through five-year blocks of riddling. Shown here are all riddle events published between 1890-1920. What do these events look like? What themes were most important to riddlers in this period?
